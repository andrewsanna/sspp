First time here?

You won't be asked to do anything — just come as you are.

Whether you've been Orthodox your whole life or are stepping into a church for the first time, here's exactly what to expect:

  • Come as you are — there's no dress code, though many dress modestly out of respect.
  • Services run about 90 minutes — feel free to arrive or leave as needed, no judgment.
  • Services blend English and Greek — printed guides are available so you can follow along.
  • Free parking right on site, with greeters at the door to help you find a seat.
