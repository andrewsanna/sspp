Member Portal

SSPP Realm

Realm is our parish's church database — built mainly to support our staff, but it also makes it easier for our church family to stay connected with each other and with parish life.

Common questions

What is SSPP Realm?

Realm is our new church database. It primarily serves the administrative needs of our staff, but it also has features that support the way you're involved in the church — helping our parish family connect with one another.

What can I see and do in SSPP Realm?

You decide how much of Realm you want to use. You can view your giving statements, make or edit contributions, connect with the ministries you're part of, and see our parish directory with photos.

How do I request and sign up for my account?

Fill in the request form to the right and it will be sent to our parish administrator. Within 48 hours you'll get an email from Sts. Peter & Paul inviting you to create an account. It will prompt you to set a password — once you log in, your account is active.

How do I get the SSPP Realm app?

Scan the QR code, or use the sign-in link, to open Realm on your phone or computer.
